Overview

Stay at the eco-friendly Sekekama Tented Camp, where fully solar-powered tents boast all the comforts of home—including beds, linens, and towels—and offer an unparalleled sleeping experience akin to what you’d expect at a 4-star city hotel. The camp features a communal area for dining, socializing, and relaxing, as well as optional amenities such as a pool, bar, or restaurant, all adding up to exceptional value for money.

Our safari packages are more economical than others because we operate our own budget lodges, safari vehicles, Kruger shuttles, and transfer vehicles. By managing all these services in-house, we avoid outsourcing costs, passing these savings directly to you.

What to Expect

Moafrika Tours proudly offers the most affordable 2-day Kruger safari in the country. This expertly crafted package is ideal for guests with only one night to spare, combining visits to both a private reserve in Greater Kruger and the iconic Kruger National Park. Our integrated approach allows us to deliver the best experiences in one seamless package.

Day 1 – Johannesburg to Kruger National Park

You will be picked up from Johannesburg’s OR Tambo International Airport and transported to Sekekama Tented Camp, where you’ll check into your glamping site by 2:00pm. After settling in and exploring the camp’s facilities, you’ll embark on a thrilling 3-hour afternoon game drive through the Greater Kruger’s Blue Canyon at 3:00pm. This open vehicle safari offers a chance to spot iconic wildlife and big 5 as well as other antelope and plains game. Upon returning to the camp around 7:00pm, enjoy a delicious meal by the boma fire and spend a serene night under the starlit sky. You may find yourself drifting off to sleep to the sounds of lions roaring or hyenas howling around the camp throughout the night. If you’re particularly fortunate, you might even hear both simultaneously. Included: Dinner.

Day 2 – Kruger National Park to Johannesburg

Start your day early with coffee and rusks before heading into Kruger National Park for a morning safari. The safari begins around 6:00am and concludes around 9:00am. During this time, enjoy a packed breakfast amidst the park’s natural beauty. After the drive, return to the camp for a quick shower before your departure. You will leave the camp and anticipate arriving back at OR Tambo International Airport between 5:00pm and 6:00pm. Included: Breakfast.

Good to Know

Guests are collected from Johannesburg OR Tambo International Airport between 7:00 and 7:30. Collections from any other Johannesburg hotels or addresses are possible at additional rates. Guests are dropped off at Johannesburg OR Tambo Airport around 17:00, other hotels or addresses can be arranged at additional rates.

MoAfrika Tours and its service providers provide cost-effective transfers for groups of 2 – 12 people per vehicle; please let us know if you prefer a private tour (additional rates may apply). Keep in mind that if you plan to fly out of OR Tambo International Airport on the last day of the safari tour, we recommend booking a domestic flight no earlier than 19:30 and an international flight no earlier than 21:00.
