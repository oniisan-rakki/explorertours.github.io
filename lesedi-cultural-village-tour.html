Overview

Lesedi Cultural Village is located in the heart of The Cradle of Humankind which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Set in the lush African bushveld on the outskirts of Johannesburg, Lesedi Cultural Village offers a unique perspective of the beliefs, folklore and traditional way of life of 5 different cultural groups in South Africa.

Enjoy a traditional song and dance show, an authentic African meal at Nyama Choma restaurant and soak up the peace and tranquility of the historic Cradle of Humankind. It’s a cultural feast in more ways than one.

What to Expect

After pick-ups, we drive to Lesedi Cultural Village which is located in world-famous The Cradle of Humankind, north of Johannesburg (about 35 to 45-minute drive from the city).

On arrival, you’re taken on a guided tour of 5 different traditional villages. This includes villages that depict the way of life of the Xhosa, Zulu, Pedi, Basotho and Ndebele communities. You’re treated to an energetic ‘song and dance show’ and listen to animated tales told by the people of Lesedi Cultural Village. You’re encouraged to join in the dancing.

After the show, enjoy a delicious meal at Nyama Choma restaurant and sample authentic African cuisine and beverages. There is an extensive choice of African dishes on the menu, including crocodile and ostrich. Nyama Choma restaurant is divided into three themed areas: North Africa, East Africa and South Africa.

At the end of an wonderful time at Lesedi Cultural Village, your guide drives you back to the city.
