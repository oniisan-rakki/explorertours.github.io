Overview

You can spend the day relaxing by the pool or on your private patio, watching animals. In the afternoon, you can go on a safari in an open game drive vehicle. The view of the beautiful Makhutsi River is a natural treasure, and your large living room that opens onto a large terrace gives you many chances to see elephants and antelopes grazing. At Karongwe River Lodge, you can enjoy delicious Pan African food in a rustic outdoor Boma or, on other evenings, in the beautiful dining room. Dining is never been more romantic than when you are eating outside under the stars.

The lodge has a large sitting area with doors that lead onto a deck, creating an ideal environment for viewing animals. A beautiful view of the Makhutsi River can also be had from the resort. The lodge offers seven suites, five of which are Luxury Accommodations, two of which are Family Suites, two of which are Executive Suites, and two of which are Presidential Suites; each of these suites has a private plunge pool. You will not only have free access to the Internet, a library, and a spa, but you will also have the peace of mind of knowing that security is present at all times. You will be pleased with the amount of space available in your suite, which has air conditioning and a private bathroom.

What to Expect

Day 1

You will be met at OR Tambo International Airport and driven to Karongwe River Lodge, arriving in time for a light lunch. Spend the next few hours relaxing by the pool or on the deck before heading out on a late afternoon safari in an open game drive vehicle. After watching the sun set, return to the lodge for dinner, which will be served either in the dining room or outside beneath the stars (weather permitting). You overnight in a luxury suite at Karongwe River Lodge and your stay includes lunch and dinner.

Day 2 to 4

You will be awakened at 5 a.m. and served hot coffee and rusks in preparation for a three-hour game drive in the reserve at sunrise. When you return to the resort, a late breakfast will be ready for you.The remainder of the day can be spent relaxing at the pool, library, spa, or lounge area. Your afternoon game drive will begin at 16:00 and end around 19:00, just in time for dinner in the dining room or outside under the stars (weather permitting).You spend the night in a deluxe suite at Karongwe River Lodge, which includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Day 5

On your final morning, you can have another early game drive while watching the sun rise, followed by a substantial breakfast before departing for Johannesburg mid-morning. You will arrive at OR Tambo International Airport in the late afternoon, around 17:00. Today’s deal includes breakfast at the resort.

If you plan to fly out of OR Tambo International Airport on the last day of the safari tour, we suggest reserving a domestic flight that departs no earlier than 19h30 and an international flight that departs no earlier than 21h00.
