Overview

Spend the day lounging by the pool or watching the wildlife from your private terrace before departing on a late afternoon safari in an open game drive vehicle. The stunning vista of the Makhutsi River is a natural gem and your expansive parlour room opening onto a large terrace provides opportunities to see elephant and antelope. Dining under the stars has never been more romantic than when you dine on Pan African cuisine in a rustic outdoor Boma or, on alternating evenings, in the elegant dining room. Choose from a vast collection of top South African and imported beers and wine, as well as a choice of spirits and soft beverages.

The lodge has a wide sitting area that opens up onto a deck, which provides the ideal environment for viewing game. The lodge also has a breath-taking view of the Makhutsi River. The lodge provides its guests with the option of staying in one of seven suites, including five Luxury Suites, two Family Suites, two Executive Suites, and two Presidential Suites, each of which features a private plunge pool. In addition to complimentary Internet access, a library, and a spa, you will have peace of mind knowing that there is security present around the clock. You will be pleased with the spaciousness of your air-conditioned suite, which also includes a private bathroom.

What to Expect

Day 1

You will be picked up from OR Tambo International Airport and transported to Karongwe River Lodge, arriving in time for a light lunch. Spend the day relaxing by the pool or on the deck before heading out on a late afternoon safari in an open game drive vehicle. After watching the sun set, return to the lodge for dinner, which will be served either in the dining room or outside beneath the stars (weather permitting). You overnight in a luxury suite at Karongwe River Lodge and your stay includes lunch and dinner.

Day 2

A 5:00 a.m. wake-up call, along with a hot coffee and rusks, will get you ready for a three-hour sunrise game drive in the reserve. A late breakfast awaits you when you return to the resort. The remainder of the day can be spent relaxing at the pool, library, spa, or lounge area. Your afternoon game drive will begin at 16:00 and end around 19:00, just in time for dinner in the dining room or outside under the stars (weather permitting). You spend the night in a deluxe suite at Karongwe River Lodge, which includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Day 3

On your final morning, you can have another early game drive while watching the sun rise, followed by a substantial breakfast before departing for Johannesburg mid-morning. You will arrive at OR Tambo International Airport in the late afternoon, around 17:00. Today’s deal includes breakfast at the resort.

Good to Know

Collection / Drop off

Collection from the Johannesburg OR Tambo Airport at 06:30am. Drop-off at the Johannesburg OR Tambo Airport from 17h00.

Please note

If you plan to fly out of OR Tambo International Airport on the last day of the safari tour, we suggest reserving a domestic flight that departs no earlier than 19h30 and an international flight that departs no earlier than 21h00.
