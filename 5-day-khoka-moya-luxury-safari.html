Overview

Khoka Moya is the ideal location for an opulent family safari vacation as kids can also enjoy a variety of activities, including junior tracking, full game drives, and more. The opulent tents at the luxury camp feature expansive outside decks that are ideal for unwinding on while taking in the expansive channels that stretch out in front of the camp.

Situated on two sides of a riverbed, the lodge provides a genuine wilderness experience without compromising comfort, safety, or elegance. The main dining area, lounge, and bar area at Khoka Moya Camp are built to mimic the pioneer- dwellings of the early gold prospectors. There is a large pool outside surrounded by lush lawns, a bird hide, and fifteen free-standing tents. In addition to private outdoor lounging spaces for unwinding and taking in the sights and sounds of the jungle, they feature en-suite bathrooms with double showers and separate toilets. The spacious lounge and eating facilities of the camp, which are made of corrugated iron and have a view of the lush grass that lead to the swimming pool.

What to Expect

Day 1

Begin your journey by leaving Johannesburg at 07:00 and heading east on the N4 highway. As you make your way to the Manyeleti Game Reserve, take in the scenery of the Highveld and Mpumalanga. The journey will take around six to seven hours, with a stop for refreshments along the way. You will arrive to Khoka Moya Tented Camp around noon and will have time to unwind and refresh in your luxury tented accommodation. You will go on your first game drive in an open safari vehicle in the afternoon, visiting the area and its various species. After watching the sun set over the African bush, you will return to camp for a great dinner and a comfortable night in your tent. Lunch and dinner are included with your stay.

Day 2 to 4

Get up early for a hot beverage and rusks and watch the sunrise on your morning game drives. Keep an eye out for nocturnal predators who are still on the hunt. You will return to camp for late breakfasts and enjoy the rest of the days to yourself. You can unwind at the camp or participate in alternative activities such as bush walks or cultural tours. You will go on game drives in the late afternoons too, hoping to see more of the Big Five and other magnificent wildlife. Back at the camp you will enjoy dinner around the fire and share stories of your sightings with fellow guests. You overnight in your luxury accommodation at Khoka Moya Tented Camp and your deal includes breakfast, lunch and dinner each day.

Day 5

On your last day, you will get up early again to go on your final game drive. Experience another beautiful sunrise and say goodbye to the animals and new friends.After a hearty breakfast at the camp, you will depart for Johannesburg, arriving at your drop-off point around 17:00. Your deal today includes breakfast at the lodge.
