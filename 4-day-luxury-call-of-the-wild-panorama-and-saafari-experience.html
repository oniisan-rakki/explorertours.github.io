Overview

This 4-day luxury safari at a budget price to the Kruger National Park, including the Panorama Route, is a fantastic way to experience the best of South Africa. The safari price is reasonable, all Kruger safaris are conducted in open safari vehicles, and the package offers a stay at the lovely Call of the Wild Lodge on the Hoedspruit Wildlife Estate, with breakfasts and dinners included.

Accommodation

The Call of the Wild Lodge is located in the Hoedspruit Wildlife estate near Hoedspruit, about 50 minutes from Orpen Gate to the Kruger National Park. Sunbird Suite, Kingfisher Corner, African Hoopoe, and Hornbill Haven are the four luxurious rooms at the Call of the Wild Lodge. Sunbird Suite has two double rooms. Each room has either a king size bed or two single beds and can thus sleep four people. The unit shares a bathroom with a bath and a shower. The Kingfisher Corner is distinct from the main house with access through a private entrance from the parking lot. The room has a queen-sized bed, an en-suite bathroom with an outside shower. Hornbill Refuge has a king-size bed that can be converted to two single beds and an en-suite bathroom with a bath only. The African Hoopoe room, like Kingfisher Corner, is separate from the main house and has a king-size bed as well as a single bed sleeper couch. There is an indoor and outdoor shower in the en-suite bathroom. All rooms have tea and coffee making equipment, a bar fridge, and a mosquito net.

What to Expect

Day 1

You will be picked up at O.R. Tambo Airport at 06:50 for the transfer to Hoedspruit where you should arrive around 13:30. Once at the lodge on Hoedspruit Wildlife Estate – an estate that values the bush and wildlife, with numerous non-dangerous game species on the property – you can go for a walk or bike ride and do a sunset drive in Moditlo in the Blue Canyon Reserve before dinner will be served. Dinner is included in your package.

Day 2

Your guide will meet you early this morning in an open safari vehicle for an exciting day trip into Kruger National Park. You will arrive at Orpen Gate at sunrise to explore Kruger’s central region. You will later enjoy a packed picnic breakfast and stop for lunch (at your own expense), at Satara Rest Camp and then continue your safari in the afternoon. In the late afternoon, you will return to Hoedspruit for another delicious dinner at the lodge. Overnight at Call of the Wild Bush Lodge. Breakfast and dinner are both included.

Day 3

Today you will take a day trip along the breath-taking Panorama Route. The Panorama Route is one of South Africa’s most visited destinations, with the roads winding up and over the Drakensberg Escarpment, leading to the Blyde River Canyon, the world’s third largest canyon, God’s Window with its spectacular views, and two magnificent waterfalls. Pinnacle Rock and Wonderview are two other highlights. You will also visit the historical town of Pilgrims Rest and have lunch in Graskop (at your own expense). You should be back at the lodge by 16:00, in time for a leisurely walk before dinner. Both breakfast and dinner are included in your package.

Day 4

This morning, you will have a delicious breakfast at the lodge before being picked up for a morning sunrise drive in Moditlo. After the trip, you will be dropped off at the Kamagelo Tourism Centre for the trip back to Johannesburg. You will return to O.R. Tambo Airport around 17:00. Breakfast is included in your package, but lunch on the way back is for your own account.
