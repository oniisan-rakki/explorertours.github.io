Overview

For over 35 years, Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge has been welcoming guests to experience the magic of an African safari. Overlooking a waterhole and an open plain, the lodge offers a unique blend of luxury, tradition, and adventure. Guests can expect to be pampered with spacious and beautifully appointed accommodations, world- class cuisine, and attentive service. But what truly sets Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge apart is its commitment to providing guests with an unforgettable safari experience. If you are looking for a truly luxurious and unforgettable 3-day safari experience, Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge is the perfect destination.

Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge offers guests a variety of luxurious accommodation options, all designed to provide maximum comfort and relaxation. The lodge’s spacious and beautifully appointed chalets and suites feature private plunge pools, king- size beds, and en-suite bathrooms with indoor and outdoor showers. Guests can relax and unwind on their private decks, while enjoying stunning views of the surrounding bushveld. The lodge also features a communal lounge, bar, and dining area, where guests can socialize and share stories of their day’s adventures. The lodge’s infinity pool is the perfect place to cool off on a hot day, while the spa offers a variety of treatments to help guests relax and rejuvenate. After a day of exploration, guests can relax and unwind in the lodge’s luxurious facilities, and savour delicious meals prepared by award-winning chefs.

What to Expect

Day 1:

Your adventure begins when you arrive at the lodge. Your transport from Johannesburg to the lodge can be arranged (shuttle, private transfer and flights) at an additional cost. You will arrive at the Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge and welcomed by friendly staff and shown to your luxury suite. After settling in and relaxing, you will hop on an open safari vehicle and embark on your first game drive in the reserve, where you will encounter amazing wildlife and nature. You will return to the lodge for a delicious dinner and a cosy night in your suite. Your deal today includes lunch and dinner.

Day 2:

Wake up early to the sounds of the bush and enjoy a hot drink and a rusk before heading out for your morning game drive. You will have a chance to see some of the nocturnal animals that are still active before dawn, as well as witness the sunrise over the horizon. You will come back to the lodge for a hearty brunch and have some free time to relax, swim, read, or do whatever you please. In the afternoon, you will go on another game drive, exploring different areas of the reserve and spotting more wildlife. You will return to the lodge for a starlit dinner and a restful night in your suite. Your deal today includes breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Day 3:

Rise early for your last game drive in the reserve, hoping to catch a glimpse of some of the animals that you might have missed before. You will enjoy one last African sunrise and then head back to the lodge for breakfast. After breakfast, it’s time to say goodbye to the lodge and head back to Johannesburg.
