Overview

Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge has been welcoming tourists to enjoy the thrill of an African safari for over 35 years. The resort, which overlooks a waterhole and an open plain, provides a unique combination of luxury, tradition, and adventure. Guests may anticipate large and elegantly designed rooms, world-class food, and dedicated service. What genuinely distinguishes Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge is its dedication to providing visitors with an outstanding safari experience. Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge is the ideal venue for a genuinely magnificent and amazing 4-day safari adventure.

The lodge provides visitors with a range of exquisite lodging alternatives, all of which are designed to give optimum comfort and relaxation. Private plunge pools, king-size beds, and en-suite bathrooms with indoor and outdoor showers are available in the lodge’s vast and elegantly decorated chalets and suites. Guests may unwind and relax on their individual terraces while taking in the views of the bushveld. The lodge also has a community lounge, bar, and dining room where guests may interact and exchange stories about their exploits for the day. The infinity pool at the resort is ideal for cooling down on a hot day, and the spa provides a range of treatments to help guests relax and revitalize. After a day of exploring, visitors may unwind in the lodge’s magnificent amenities and enjoy exquisite meals made by award-winning chefs.

What to Expect

Day 1:

Your adventure begins when you arrive at the lodge. Your transport from Johannesburg to the lodge can be arranged (shuttle, private transfer and flights) at an additional cost. You will arrive at the Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge, where you will be welcomed by friendly staff and shown to your luxury suite. After settling in and relaxing, you will hop on an open safari vehicle and embark on your first game drive in the reserve, where you will encounter amazing wildlife and nature. You will return to the lodge for a delicious dinner and a cosy night in your suite. Your deal today includes lunch and dinner.

Day 2 to 3:

Wake up early and enjoy a hot refreshment before heading out for your morning game drive. Enjoy the opportunity to see some of the nocturnal animals that are still active before dawn, as well as witness the sunrise over the horizon. Back at the lodge you can have a hearty brunch and then enjoy some free time to relax, swim, read, or do whatever you please. In the afternoon, you will go on another game drive, exploring different areas of the reserve and spotting more wildlife. You will return to the lodge for a starlit dinner and a restful night in your suite. Your deal today includes breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Day 4:

Rise early for your last game drive in the reserve, hoping to catch a glimpse of some of the animals that you might have missed before. You will enjoy one last African sunrise and then head back to the lodge for breakfast. After breakfast, it’s time to say goodbye to the lodge and head back to Johannesburg.
