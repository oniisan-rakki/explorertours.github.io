Overview

For more than 35 years, Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge has welcomed visitors who want to experience the excitement of an African safari. The resort offers a distinctive fusion of luxury, history, and adventure, with views of a waterhole and an open plain. They can count on spacious, tastefully decorated accommodations, top-notch cuisine, and attentive service. Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge is distinguished by its commitment to giving guests a fantastic safari experience. The ideal setting for a truly stunning and fantastic 5-day safari excursion is Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge.

The exquisite lodging choices available to visitors at Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge are all created to maximize comfort and relaxation. The lodge’s large, exquisitely decorated chalets and suites with king-size beds, private plunge pools, and attached bathrooms with indoor and outdoor showers. On their personal decks, guests may unwind and enjoy the wonderful views of the surrounding bushland. Additionally, the lodge has a common lounge, bar, and dining room where visitors may mingle and tell tales of their day’s activities. On sweltering days, the lodge’s infinity pool is the ideal spot to cool down, while the spa provides a range of treatments to assist visitors unwind and recover. After a day of exploring, visitors may repose in the lodge’s opulent amenities and enjoy award- winning chefs; delectable cuisine.

What to Expect

Day 1:

Your adventure begins when you arrive at the lodge. Your transport from Johannesburg to the lodge can be arranged (shuttle, private transfer and flights) at an additional cost. You will arrive at the Sabi Sabi Bush Lodge, where you will be welcomed by friendly staff and shown to your luxury suite. After settling in and relaxing, you will hop on an open safari vehicle and embark on your first game drive in the reserve, where you will encounter amazing wildlife and nature. You will return to the lodge for a delicious dinner and a cosy night in your suite. Your deal today includes lunch and dinner.

Day 2 to 4:

Rise early and enjoy a hot refreshment before heading out for your morning game drive. Look for some of the nocturnal animals that are still active before dawn, as well as witness the sunrise over the horizon. Back at the lodge you can have a hearty brunch and then enjoy some free time to relax, swim, read, or do whatever you please. In the afternoon, you will go on another game drive, exploring different areas of the reserve and spotting more wildlife. You will return to the lodge for a starlit dinner and a restful night in your suite. Your deal includes breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Day 5:

Get up early for your last game drive in the reserve, hoping to catch a glimpse of some of the animals that you might have missed before. You will enjoy one last African sunrise and then head back to the lodge for breakfast. After breakfast, it’s time to say goodbye to the lodge and head back to Johannesburg.
