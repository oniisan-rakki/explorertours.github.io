Overview

You open your eyes to a breath-taking view of the unspoiled wilderness, teeming with a diverse array of species. This is the daily experience of guests of the 5-day safari at Londolozi Tree Camp, a luxury safari lodge perched high in the branches of ancient ebony trees. Embark on thrilling game drives in open-sided vehicles, led by knowledgeable guides and trackers. Witness the Big Five in their natural habitat, alongside other iconic African animals. Learn about the intricate relationships between the species and the delicate balance of nature in the African bushveld. Londolozi Tree Camp offers the ultimate African safari experience, with its luxurious accommodations, world-class game drives, and stunning scenery. Reconnect with nature and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Perched on stilts among Jackalberry and Leadwood trees, Londolozi Tree Camp offers panoramic views of the Sand River and its wildlife. Elevated, lantern-lit walkways connect the suites, creating a sense of adventure. Elegantly decorated to blend with the natural surroundings, the luxurious suites feature spacious bathrooms with indoor and outdoor showers, king-size beds with mosquito nets, air-conditioning and fans, private lounge areas with minibars, and private decks with plunge pools. Indulge in the finer things in life while immersing yourself in the wilderness. Londolozi Tree Camp is a sanctuary where you can relax, rejuvenate, and reconnect with nature.

What to Expect

Day 1

Your adventure begins when you arrive at the lodge. Your transport from Johannesburg to the lodge can be arranged (shuttle, private transfer and flights) at an additional cost. Once at the lodge, you’ll be greeted with a warm welcome and a refreshing drink. After checking into your luxurious suite, you’ll have time to relax and unwind before embarking on your first afternoon game drive. As the sun dips below the horizon, you’ll set out in an open safari vehicle in search of Africa’s iconic wildlife. Your expert guide will lead you through the reserve, sharing their knowledge of the bushveld and its inhabitants. After a thrilling day of safari, you’ll return to the lodge for a sumptuous dinner under the stars.

Days 2-4

Enjoy a hot beverage and rusks on your private deck before embarking on a morning game drive. As the sun rises over the horizon, you’ll have the chance to witness the bushveld come to life. After your game drive, return to the lodge for a leisurely brunch. The rest of the day is yours to relax and enjoy the amenities of the lodge. In the afternoon, embark on another game drive with your expert guide. After a long day of exploring, return to the lodge for a delicious dinner under the stars. Enjoy the company of other guests and staff as you share stories and laughter.

Day 5

On your final day at Londolozi Tree Camp, wake up early for one last morning game drive. After your game drive, return to the lodge for a hearty breakfast. Then, it’s time to bid farewell to Londolozi Tree Camp and embark on your journey back to Johannesburg. You’ve had the chance to encounter some of the world’s most magnificent wildlife and experience the beauty of the bushveld.
